Emma J O'Brien

University of Pittsburgh Medical Center, Pittsburgh, PA.

About

Emma J O'Brien is Dr. Katalin Szanto's Lead Research Project Coordinator in the Longitudinal Research Program in Late-Life Suicide (LRP) and the Rhythms after Loss for Improving Suicide Risk in Elders (RISE) study at the University of Pittsburgh Medical Center's Western Psychiatric Hospital. She is hoping to begin a Clinical Psychology Doctoral Program beginning Fall 2024. Emma has one publication and several ongoing projects in addition to several posters - feel free to take a look below. Emma is open to email inquiries regarding her work or if you are interested in collaboration.
